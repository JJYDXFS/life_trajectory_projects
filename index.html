Charting Life Trajectory at Scale: A Trilogy 📚

This website presents our research efforts on modeling human life trajectories—how people move 🚶, evolve 🌱, and interact 🧑‍🤝‍🧑 across time and space—through large-scale data. Across a trilogy of papers, we explore how life paths can be extracted, compared, and connected at unprecedented scale. Understanding such trajectories not only reveals behavioral and mobility patterns, but also deepens our grasp of the social fabric that connects lives. Enjoy!

— The Authors

Project Overview

  • 📘 Our first work presents an early attempt to extract life trajectories from textual materials
  • 📗 The second work introduces a general extraction framework, yielding million-level trajectories from Wikipedia
  • 📙 Building on life trajectory, our third work explore the intersections of life trajectories
